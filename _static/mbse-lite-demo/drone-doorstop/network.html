sys-1: Charge duration

A battery with <5% capacity shall charge to >95% capacity in <5 hours.**Rationale**

sys-2: Charge indicator location

The charge indicator shall be located 1.0 +/- 0.2 cm from the charging port.**Rationale**

org-1: Charging

A user should be able to quickly charge the device with a cell phone charger. **Rationale**

ver-1: Re-charging

1. Connect a device with a low battery to the charger. 1. Confirm charge completes within the specified time limit. 1. Confirm the displays per the diagram. 1. Connect a device with a low battery to the charger. 1. Disconnect the battery prior to the battery completely charging. 1. Confirm the displays per the diagram. **Rationale**

    repeat
    split
    :[fnc-1.1] Charge device;
    split again
        :Blink charge indicator yellow;
    end split
    repeat while (Charge complete?) is (no)
    -> yes;
    :Charge indicator green;
end fork
end
