Consultant for hire, super-resolution imaging and giga-pixel camera design. Also open to permanent positions.

Expertise and Services
--------------------------

**What I do (aka the applications)**

* New product development (NPD) and prototyping.

* High-throughput, super-resolution bio-imaging system & analysis pipeline.

* Scientific & industrial camera system hardware & firmware.

* Motion control systems for microscope auto-leveling and auto-focusing.

**Where I apply (aka the capabilities)**

* End-to-end system architecture & engineering, e.g. requirement traceability
  analysis, trade studies, and design thinking.

* Firmware development and QC, e.g. C++17, real-time systems, GPU acceleration.

* Illumination/imaging optics and imaging hardware design.

* Scientific graphical user interface design.

* Algorithm design and development, e.g. super-resolution, deconvolution, data analysis.

* Algorithm porting and GPU acceleration.
